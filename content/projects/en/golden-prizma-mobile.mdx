## The problem

Customers wanted the platform on their phones — accounts, orders, and shipping updates without opening a browser, with a nudge the moment something changed.

## What I built

A Flutter app for iOS and Android on the same backend as the web platform, with Firebase push for shipping and account notifications so customers hear about updates the moment they happen.
