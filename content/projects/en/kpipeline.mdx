## The problem

Kurd Pipeline (KPL) is an industrial engineering and fabrication company in Erbil — pipelines, storage tanks, structural steel, inspection, and maintenance. They needed a corporate site that conveyed scale and credibility — ISO certification, decades of work — to industrial clients across Iraq and the region.

## What I built

A corporate site on Next.js: clear sections for each service line, a project portfolio, and credentials kept front and center. React and TypeScript throughout, Tailwind for styling.

## My role

Solo — design and development, end to end.
