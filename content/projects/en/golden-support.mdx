## The problem

Support was scattered across channels. Customers on the web and in the mobile app needed one place to raise issues, and staff needed a single dashboard to answer them in real time — no missed messages, no switching tools.

## What I built

A chat-based ticketing system with live messaging over WebSockets, connected to the mobile app and a dedicated agent dashboard. Tickets, conversations, and history in one place, updating instantly on both sides.

## My role

Solo — full-stack, including the real-time layer.
