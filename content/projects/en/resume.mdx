## The problem

Building a good CV is hard — and harder in Kurdistan, where most tools are English-only and don't handle Kurdish text or right-to-left layout. People end up fighting Word templates.

## What I built

An AI-powered resume builder: it drafts and refines CV content, offers multiple themes, and exports ATS-friendly resumes in both English and Kurdish. React with TanStack Start on the front end, Go on the back.

## My role

Founder — product direction, design, and the full stack from the Go backend to the React front end.
