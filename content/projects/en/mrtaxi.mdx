## The problem

Mr Taxi is a ride-hailing platform for Iraq — riders, drivers, live trips, payments — in the mould of Uber. The hard parts aren't the screens: real-time driver tracking, geospatial matching, routing, and payments all have to stay reliable at city scale.

## What I built

I led the project as team lead and project manager, and built its core. Geo and location services in Go with Tile38 for fast geospatial queries; the real-time layer over WebSockets; the overall system architecture; and the DevOps. Laravel and a React dashboard on top, PostgreSQL with PostGIS for spatial data, Redis for caching, and payment integrations.
