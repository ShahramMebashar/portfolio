## The problem

IOM's Enterprise Development Fund backs small and medium enterprises in Iraq — providing capital to businesses in high-labour sectors to drive rapid, large-scale job creation. The programme needed a platform to run on: managing enterprises, applications, and the data behind funding decisions across primary, secondary, and tertiary sectors.

## What I built

As a full-stack developer at Awrosoft, I built the EDF platform on Laravel with an Inertia and Vue 3 front end — the workflows and screens the programme runs on day to day, backed by MySQL and Redis.

## My role

Full-stack developer at Awrosoft ICT — front end and back end, as part of the team delivering for IOM.
