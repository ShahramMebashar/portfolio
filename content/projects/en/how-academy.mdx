## The problem

HOW Academy is a training and consultancy provider running industry-focused programmes from its centres. It needed a site that presented those programmes credibly and turned interest into enrolments — kept current by the academy without developer help.

## What I built

As a full-stack developer at Awrosoft, I built the HOW Academy site on Laravel with Livewire and Vue 3 on the front end and Tailwind for styling, backed by MySQL and Redis — programme listings, centre information, and content the team manages itself.

Full-stack developer at Awrosoft ICT — front end and back end, end to end.
