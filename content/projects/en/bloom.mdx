## The problem

Project Bloom is a Kurdistan Regional Government initiative, run with IOM, offering accessible business loans — up to IQD 150 million per business, with no collateral required — to help businesses across the region grow. It needed a public platform where owners could understand the programme and apply.

## What I built

As a full-stack developer at Awrosoft, I built the Project Bloom platform on Laravel with MySQL, styled with Tailwind — the public-facing site and the application workflow behind the loan programme.

## My role

Full-stack developer at Awrosoft ICT — front end and back end, delivering for the KRG and IOM.
