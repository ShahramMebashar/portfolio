## The problem

Segment is a Kurdistan investment corporation spanning four very different sectors — investments, defence, oil & gas, and manufacturing. They needed one corporate site that presented that breadth credibly to investors and partners, without feeling scattered.

## What I built

A corporate site on Next.js with Supabase behind it: a clear section for each business sector, server-rendered for SEO and fast loads. TypeScript throughout, Tailwind for styling.

## My role

Solo — design and development, end to end.
