## The problem

The local store needed its own point-of-sale and stock control — but not as a separate island. Sales and inventory had to stay in sync with the main Golden Prizma platform, not drift apart.

## What I built

A dedicated POS with inventory tracking, connected to the main app so stock levels and sales reconcile in one place. Same Laravel and Inertia/React stack, MySQL, Redis, and S3.

## My role

Solo — full-stack, end to end.
