[Amazing Architecture](https://amazingarchitecture.com) is an international publication covering contemporary architecture — studios, projects, products. I designed and built the whole platform: the public site and the Laravel-based CMS the editorial team uses every day.

Off-the-shelf CMS tools couldn't handle what they needed — image-heavy project pages, multiple authors, taxonomy across studios, architects, countries and project types, and pages that had to stay fast when a feature went viral. So I built one.

Redis caches the hot article pages and taxonomy lookups; the media pipeline handles high-res photography from upload through CDN delivery. Solo project — design, database, backend, frontend, deploy, and the maintenance that followed.
