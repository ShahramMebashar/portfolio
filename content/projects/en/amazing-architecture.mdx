**Year** &nbsp; 2022
**Live** &nbsp; [amazingarchitecture.com](https://amazingarchitecture.com)

## The problem

The editorial team needed to publish image-heavy architecture features daily — multi-author flow, taxonomy across studios, architects, and countries, and pages that stayed fast when a feature went viral. Off-the-shelf CMSes couldn't handle it.

## What I built

A Laravel CMS purpose-fit to the editorial workflow: rich media, drag-and-drop ordering, taxonomy for studios and architects. The public Vue.js site with Redis-cached rendering. A media pipeline from upload through CDN delivery.

## My role

Solo — design, database, backend, frontend, deploy, and the maintenance that followed.
