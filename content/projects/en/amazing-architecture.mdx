## Overview

[Amazing Architecture](https://amazingarchitecture.com) is an international publication showcasing contemporary architecture projects, studios, and products from around the world. I designed and developed the entire platform end-to-end — from visual design through the custom content management system that powers daily editorial publishing.

## The problem

Off-the-shelf CMS platforms couldn't handle the editorial team's workflow: rich media-heavy project pages, multi-author contributions, taxonomy across architects, studios, countries, and categories, and fast page loads under heavy traffic spikes when featured stories went viral.

## What I built

- **Custom CMS (Laravel + Vue.js SPA admin):** purpose-built editor for architecture articles with image galleries, embedded media, rich metadata, drag-and-drop ordering, and a taxonomy system for studios, architects, locations, and project types.
- **Public website:** responsive front-end optimized for image-heavy editorial content, server-rendered for SEO, with category, studio, and search browsing.
- **Performance layer:** Redis caching for hot article pages, rendered fragments, and taxonomy queries — keeping response times low even during traffic surges.
- **Media pipeline:** uploads, resizing, and CDN-ready delivery for high-resolution architectural photography.

## Role

Sole designer and developer — UI design, database modeling, backend architecture, front-end implementation, deployment, and ongoing maintenance.

## Stack

**Backend:** Laravel, MySQL, Redis  ·  **Frontend:** Vue.js  ·  **Infra:** CDN-backed media delivery, Redis-cached rendering
