## The problem

BIM is a multidisciplinary infrastructure company in Erbil — construction, government tenders, services, and procurement — with a growing portfolio of large public projects. They needed a corporate site that presented that work credibly to government and private clients, published news as projects progressed, and worked across three languages.

## What I built

A full-stack site on Laravel with a Vue + Inertia front end, plus a custom CMS the team uses to manage the project portfolio and publish news — all trilingual (English, Kurdish, Arabic). Redis handles caching; MySQL backs the content.

## My role

Solo — design, backend, frontend, the multilingual setup, and deployment.
