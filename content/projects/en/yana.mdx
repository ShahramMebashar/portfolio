## The problem

You're Not Alone (YANA), a Rwanga Foundation project, works to rehabilitate and empower orphaned children in Iraq — psychologically, educationally, and intellectually. Delivering that care at scale needs a real management system, not spreadsheets: tracking daily tasks, activities, and each child's progress.

## What I built

As a full-stack developer at Awrosoft, I built the YANA management system on Laravel with a React and TypeScript front end, styled with Tailwind and backed by MySQL and Redis — daily tasks, activities, and the records the team relies on, designed to scale toward managing orphanages across the country.

## My role

Full-stack developer at Awrosoft ICT — front end and back end, delivering for the Rwanga Foundation.
