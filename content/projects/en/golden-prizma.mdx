## The problem

Golden Prizma is a trading company moving goods across many branches. Customers needed their own secure accounts — either self-registered or set up with help from staff — and the business needed shipping tracked across every branch in one place, not scattered across spreadsheets and phone calls.

## What I built

A customer platform with a hardened account system — self-registration plus staff-assisted onboarding — and a global shipping system that ties the branches together: orders, statuses, and tracking in one flow. Laravel and Inertia/React, MySQL for data, Redis for caching and queues, S3 for files.

## My role

Solo — full-stack, every layer, on my own.
