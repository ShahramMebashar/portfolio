## The problem

The Institute for Research and Development (IRDK) needed an institutional site to publish its work — news, activities, and research — in one place, kept current by staff without touching code.

## What I built

As a full-stack developer at Awrosoft, I built the IRDK site on Laravel with Livewire and Vue 3 on the front end and Tailwind for styling, backed by MySQL and Redis — content sections for news, activities, and research, manageable by the institute's team.

## My role

Full-stack developer at Awrosoft ICT — front end and back end, end to end.
