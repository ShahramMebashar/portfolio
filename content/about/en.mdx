## Who I Am

I'm Shaho — a full-stack developer who turns ideas into products. I work with startups and businesses to build MVPs, web apps, and mobile apps that actually ship.

My toolkit spans the full stack: **Go** and **Laravel** on the backend, **React** and **Vue** on the frontend, **Flutter** for mobile, and everything in between — databases, APIs, DevOps, the works.
